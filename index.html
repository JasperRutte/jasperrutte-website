Who am I?

My name is Jasper Rutte, I'm a 17-year-old student that has recently started his vocational education as a software developer at Da Vinci College in Dordrecht. Most of my life I've been interested in computer related things, such as hardware and software. A few years ago I started to take this interest more seriously and my fascination for programming software skyrocketed!. Because of my peaked interest in programming, I chose for my vocational education at Da Vinci College in Dordrecht. The school has good teachers, a good curriculum, and a pleasant overall vibe. These are a few of the reasons I chose Da Vinci College.

Programming experiences

Most of my experience with programming has been making projects at school and making smaller projects at home. A few projects I'm pretty proud of are: A Wordle like game with colors using JS, an adventure game using Python and this website using Tailwind CSS. I have most experience with those 3 programming languages, but I can also get around with PHP. My thinking and problem-solving abilities are also growing quite fast. Even tho I'm just a starting developer, I think my ability to do those two things are quite good. I love making website designs and making them into an actual reality. The best part about programming for me is walking into obstacles and overcoming them. The more obstacles, the more I learn.
